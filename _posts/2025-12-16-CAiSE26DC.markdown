UGent Business Informatics professor Geert Poels is one of the chairs of the CAiSE 2026 Doctoral Consortium. UGent Business Informatics has a strong connection with CAiSE and its DC, e.g., visiting professor Steven Mertens not only participated but also received the PhD award, resulting in the publication of his PhD dissertation as a Springer LNBIP volume.

See [CAiSE 2026 DC](https://caise26.polimi.it/?page_id=328.html) for the call for papers.
